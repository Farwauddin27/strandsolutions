Essential oils

Some essential oils, such as rosemary, lavender, and peppermint, have shown effectiveness in promoting hair growth.
You can mix a few drops of these oils with a carrier oil (like coconut oil) and massage it into your scalp.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera gel has soothing properties and can promote hair growth. Cut open an aloe vera leaf, scoop out the gel,
and apply it to your scalp. Leave it on for about 45 minutes before rinsing with plain water. Repeat this mask 3-4
times a week for best results.

Minoxidil

This antihypertensive vasodilator is commonly used for both men and women. It helps prevent further hair loss and,
in some cases, stimulates hair growth.
