PREMATURE BALDING

1.Gentle Hair Care

Avoid aggressive brushing, tight hairstyles, and excessive heat styling. Use mild hair care products.

2.Scalp Massage

Regular scalp massages may improve blood circulation and promote healthier hair.

3.Medical Treatment

The first step of premature baldness treatment is to get a check-up with a reputed doctor. The doctor will conduct tests on your scalp and diagnose the cause of baldness. The doctor will also conduct tests that check thyroid and hormone levels. The treatment is then based on the reason for your hair fall.
