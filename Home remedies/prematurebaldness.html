Rosemary Oil Scalp Massage

  • Rosemary oil has been traditionally used to stimulate hair growth, improve circulation in the scalp.
  • Mix a few drops of rosemary essential oil with a carrier oil like coconut oil or olive oil.
  • Gently massage the mixture into your scalp for 5-10 minutes, ensuring it covers the scalp.
  • Leave it on for at least 30 minutes or overnight for better absorption.
  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo.
  • Repeat this process a few times a week.

    • Green Tea Rinse

  • Green tea is rich in antioxidants that may help prevent hair loss and promote hair growth.
  • Brew a strong cup of green tea and allow it to cool down to room temperature.
  • After shampooing your hair, pour the cooled green tea over your scalp and hair.
  • Massage it gently into your scalp for a few minutes.
  • Leave it on for about 5-10 minutes, then rinse it out with cool water. You can do this rinse once or twice a week.
