Graying Home Remedies

Curry Leaves and Coconut Oil

  • Curry leaves are rich in antioxidants and Vitamin B
  • Blend or grate an onion and extract its juice using a fine strainer or cheesecloth.Take a handful of fresh curry leaves and wash them thoroughly.
  • Heat some coconut oil in a pan and add the curry leaves to it. Let the leaves cook in the oil until they become charred.
  • Strain the oil and allow it to cool down to a comfortable temperature. Massage this oil onto your scalp and hair, focusing on areas with graying hair.
  • Leave it on for at least an hour, and then wash it off with a mild shampoo. Repeat this process regularly for best results.

    • Black Tea Rinse

  • Black tea contains tannins that can help darken hair and cover gray strands.
  • Brew a strong cup of black tea by steeping several tea bags in hot water for at least 30 minutes.
  • Allow the tea to cool down to room temperature.
  • After shampooing your hair, pour the cooled black tea over your hair, making sure to saturate it thoroughly.
  • Leave the tea on your hair for about 15-20 minutes before rinsing it out with lukewarm water.
  • You can do this rinse once or twice a week to gradually darken graying hair.
