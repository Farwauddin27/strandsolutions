Hair Growth Home Remedies

Onion Juice Treatment

  • Onions contain sulfur, which is known to increase circulation and stimulate hair follicles, promoting hair growth.
  • Blend or grate an onion and extract its juice using a fine strainer or cheesecloth.
  • Apply the onion juice onto your scalp using a cotton pad or applicator.
  • Leave it on for about 15-30 minutes, then rinse it off
  • Repeat this process 2-3 times a week for a few months to potentially see improvements in hair growth.

    • Cocunut oil Massage

  • Coconut oil is rich in nutrients such as vitamins E and K, and iron, which are beneficial for hair growth.
  • Warm up some coconut oil and massage it gently onto your scalp in circular motions.
  • Leave it on for at least 30 minutes to an hour, or even overnight for better absorption.
  • Wash it off with a mild shampoo. Regular application can help nourish the scalp and strengthen hair follicles, potentially promoting hair growth.
